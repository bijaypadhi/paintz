About PaintKFZ

What is PaintKFZ?

PaintKFZ (pronounced like “paints”) is a simple program for creating and editing drawings and other images, designed with Chromebooks in mind, but usable in any modern browser.

PaintKFZ is fast and easy to use, with a touch- and mouse-friendly Material Design interface, full offline support, and no plug-ins required. It is particularly designed to feel similar to tools such as Microsoft Paint and KolourPaint. And because it is web-based, you can try it your browser without installing anything. If you have been looking for MS Paint for your Chromebook, PaintKFZ is your solution.

What isn't PaintKFZ?

Because PaintKFZ is intended to be an MS Paint alternative for platforms that do not have one built in (such as Chrome OS and MacOS), it is not designed to support more powerful artist features, such as layers, advanced brushes, or filters. Minimizing the number of features in PaintKFZ helps keep it simple and fast, but also, multiple advanced web-based image editing programs already exist—if you are looking for Photoshop for your Chromebook, I highly recommend Photopea.

What can I do with PaintKFZ?

Just about anything you can do in MS Paint on a Windows PC, you can do in PaintKFZ on a Chromebook. Pencil, brush, curve tool, freeform selection, it's all there. There are still a few features in the works, but they will hopefully be available soon enough.

What does it cost?

Absolutely nothing—PaintKFZ is free to use, and it always will be. It is supported by generous donors on Patreon and Ko-fi. If PaintKFZ has been useful to you, I hope you'll consider buying me a cuppa.

Does it sell my data?

Nope. PaintKFZ is accessed through your web browser, but it runs on your device—nothing personal, including the images you edit, gets uploaded or stored in the “cloud”. For more details, see the PaintKFZ privacy policy.
